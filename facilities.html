Modern Infrastructure & Security

  • Well-furnished, spacious building with adequate seating, ventilation, and light to facilitate stress-free dealings with customers.
  • The building covers an area of about 25,000 square feet across 4 floors, with all sections fully air-conditioned.
  • A modern lift facility has been installed for the convenience of all members.
  • The Ground Floor is dedicated to vehicle parking for the convenience of customers.
  • From a security perspective, the bank is protected by a complete CC camera system, installed sirens, and night security guards.
  • A note-counting machine and a fake note detection machine have been installed for customer convenience.
  • A highly secure, separate safe locker system is available for customers.

Agricultural & Farmer Support

  • Agricultural chemical fertilizers are distributed to farmer customers on loan for a period of one month.
  • Loan facility at 0% interest rate available for agriculture purpose loans (power tillers, tractors, grass-cutting machines, etc.).
  • A program recognizing and honoring the 4 best farmers in the field has been successfully running for the past ten years.
  • Under the Farmers' Pension Scheme, a monthly pension of Rs. 1,000 is provided to eligible farmers.
  • Special Information Camps and Workshops are organized every month for the educational benefit of farmers.
  • The system provides crop loans through the farmers' revolving scheme.

Financial & Member Benefits

  • A 25% dividend has been consistently given to the members of the bank for the last 20 years.
  • All families residing in Moodbidire - Pranthya and Marpadi village hold membership with this bank.
  • A loan facility at a 5% interest rate is available for bank members to adopt solar water heaters and solar lights.
  • Education loans are provided to bank members for their children's education at a 10% interest rate.
  • Members who pay loan installments on time are given a 1% discount on interest (Exceptions apply).
  • Remarkably, 90% loan recovery has been achieved without any legal action or court involvement.
  • Complete waiver of overdue interest on all loans was enacted as part of the Centenary celebrations.

Health & Welfare Initiatives

  • Medical assistance scheme of Rs. 15,000 is available for members suffering from kidney problems, heart problems, cancer, etc.
  • Dialysis facilities are available through Alva's Health Center at special concessional rates for members and their dependents.
  • Upon the death of a bank member, a scheme provides Rs. 12,000 from the death fund to their heirs.
  • All bank members and their families are registered under the State Government's Yashasvini Vima Yojana.
  • Mediclaim and accident insurance arrangements are actively maintained for all bank employees and Pigmy collectors.
  • In memory of the Centenary year, 2 Ventilators and 1 Dialysis Machine were donated to Alva's Hospital.

Community & Social Impact

  • Distribution of ration to about 3,500 cardholders through a computerized billing system, complete with roofing and seating facilities.
  • A separate delivery system ensures ration cardholders can receive their items on all days of the respective month.
  • A program is organized annually to recognize and honor active senior members.
  • Every year, students of the Co-operative Training Center who secure ranks are awarded by the Bank.
  • Prize money of Rs. 750 is awarded to students in local schools who score the highest marks in Kannada and Hindi (7th to 10th class).
  • An annual incentive subsidy of Rs. 2,000 is given to help mentally challenged children.
  • In cases of national calamities (earthquakes, floods, Covid-19), the highest amount of donation is given to the Relief Funds.
  • Rs. 10 lakh was provided for the renovation of the public cemetery under our jurisdiction at Moodbidiri.
  • Financial assistance is collected and sent for religious programs (Renovation, Brahmakalasha, Mastakabhisheka, etc.).

Co-operative Sector & Staff Support

  • Rs. 1,000 is provided to all Retired staff of the Bank. The pension fund is invested in LIC for direct pension disbursement to retired employees.
  • A borewell was dug to provide a dedicated water system to the Cooperative Training Centre.
  • Essay writing competitions are organized for Co-operative Training Centre and Dhavala College students.
  • A dedicated program recognizes and honors well-functioning cooperative societies and their office bearers in the Dakshina Kannada district.
  • The Sahakar Kalpavriksha Award has been given continuously for the past 6 years to those rendering excellent service in the co-operative sector.
  • Quarterly celebrations are held to create awareness of cooperation among customers and make them more active.
