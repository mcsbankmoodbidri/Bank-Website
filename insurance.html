Kalpavriksha Aarogya Card

Health is your greatest wealth. At MCS Bank, we are deeply committed to the well-being and security of our community members. The Kalpavriksha Aarogya Card is our premier health initiative designed to provide substantial financial relief during critical medical emergencies.

Medical emergencies can strike without warning, often bringing significant financial strain alongside emotional stress. By subscribing to the Aarogya Card, our members secure a vital safety net that helps cover the heavy costs associated with surgical procedures and hospitalizations.

We have partnered with a vast network of healthcare providers to ensure that you receive the best possible care, wherever you are in the country. Our streamlined claim settlement process guarantees that the focus remains entirely on recovery, rather than financial burdens.

Important Guidelines

  • This scheme is limited to 'A' class members of the association.
  • The fee to be paid to get a health card is Rs. 1000.
  • This facility is limited to surgery only.
  • You can get treatment at any hospital inside the country.
  • A fixed package for various diseases will be paid by the Sangha.
  • Original bills and Discharge summary are required.
  • Max benefit per year is 2 Lakhs only.
